Scrambled Eggs

Here is a recipe for scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • one tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge to the center of the pan every 20 seconds (as if you were making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
